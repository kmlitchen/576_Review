Info

This app helps you record and discover public structural art— sculptures, statues, and installations—especially pieces tucked away in parks or corners of buildings.

  • Use the circular Find my location button to zoom to you.
  • Use Add Sculpture to submit a piece of art via a Survey123 form.
  • Points in the map show details when selected.

The list on the right shows known artwork locations inside the current map view.
